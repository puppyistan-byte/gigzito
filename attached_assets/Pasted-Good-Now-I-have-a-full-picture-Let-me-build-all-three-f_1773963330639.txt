What's New — Activity & Engagement Tracking
Profile View Tracking
Every time a logged-in user visits another provider's public profile page, it's silently recorded. You won't track your own views — only distinct other users count.

Comment Likes
Users can now like individual comments on any video. The toggle endpoint adds or removes their like, and the like count updates in real time.

Who Loved Me
The existing "love vote" system already tracked who cast love votes — it just had no way to surface that data to the profile owner. Now it does.

Activity Feed Page — /activity
Accessible from the navbar menu under Activity Feed. Shows a unified, reverse-chronological list of:

Event	Icon	Badge
Someone viewed your profile	👁 Blue eye	VIEW
Someone showed you love	❤️ Pink heart	LOVE
Someone liked a comment on your video	❤️ Red heart	LIKE
Filter tabs across the top: All / Views / Love / Likes
Each event shows the actor's avatar, display name or @username, what they did, and time ago
Refreshes automatically every 30 seconds in the background
Empty state with context-sensitive messages per filter
