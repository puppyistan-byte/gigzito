## The Gigness Card: Technical Spec
### 1. The Visual Profile (The "Rolodex" View)
The card is a single, high-impact surface. No flipping required for the initial browse.

Visuals: A sharp Profile Pic + the User’s Slogan (e.g., "Looking for a Marketing Collab").

The Engage Button: This is the trigger.

GZLurkers: Clicking this pops the $2/mo GZ2 upgrade modal.

GZ2 Users: Opens the Interaction Tray.

### 2. The Interaction Tray (Bot-Moderated)
When a GZ2 user hits "Engage," they get two options:

Emoji Reactions: Quick-tap sentiment (🔥, 🤝, 👋).

Direct Message: A text field for "The Pitch."

The Switchboard Scrub: Before the message hits the recipient's inbox, the GZ-Bot runs a regex and AI filter to strip out profanity, PII (if not allowed), and non-PG content.

### 3. The Digital "Master Card" (The Vault)
While the Rolodex card is simple, the Digital Master Card (accessed via the unique QR code) holds the "trimmings":

Unique QR Code: Every card has one. This is what's scanned at a physical business.

The 6-Pic Lifestyle Gallery: Revealed once the user engages or views the full profile.

Metrics: We track how many "Engage" clicks each card gets.
